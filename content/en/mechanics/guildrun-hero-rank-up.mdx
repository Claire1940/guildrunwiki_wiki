<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Guildrun Hero Rank Up Process">

Achieving S-Rank requires a systematic approach to drafting and economy management. Randomly buying units will dilute your pool and leave your team under-leveled. Follow this structured progression path to consistently rank up your primary carry units.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Your Core Carry">
    Select a starter hero (such as Yuna) and focus your initial shard purchases entirely on their duplicates. Do not buy secondary units until your main damage dealer reaches at least B-Rank.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage Shop Rerolls and Freezes">
    Reroll the shop sparingly in Act 1. If you find a duplicate of your carry, purchase it immediately. If you are short on shards, use the freeze option to lock the shop state for the next round.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maximize Campfire Events">
    Campfires are rare nodes that allow you to upgrade a hero's rank for free or at a highly discounted shard rate. Always path toward campfires when your primary carry is close to a rank threshold.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock S-Rank and Item Synergy">
    Once your carry hits S-Rank, equip them with items that scale with their newly unlocked passives. For example, S-Rank Yuna gains massive omnivamp, making high-attack-speed items incredibly potent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Campfires provide unique utility depending on the current rank of your target hero. Knowing when to use them is key to saving precious shard resources.

| Campfire Tier | Allowed Target Rank | Shard Savings | Bonus Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Camp** | C-Rank to B-Rank | ~15 Shards | +10% Base Magic/Attack |
| **Refined Camp** | B-Rank to A-Rank | ~35 Shards | +15% Base Health |
| **Exalted Camp** | A-Rank to S-Rank | ~60 Shards | +20% All Primary Stats |

<Callout type="warning" title="Reroll Trap">
Avoid aggressively rerolling the shop in the early game. Every reroll costs shards that could otherwise be saved to secure a guaranteed rank-up or purchase high-tier relics.
</Callout>
