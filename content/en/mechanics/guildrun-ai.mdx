<Section icon={Target} title="The Ultimate Poison-Crit Build Guide">

The most "broken" build currently available in **Guildrun AI** is the Poison-Crit Assassin setup. This build relies on the interaction where critical hits inflict poison, and poison enemies take increased damage from critical strikes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Yuna Early">
    Pick Yuna as your starter or find her in the first shop. Her **Shuriken Shadow Strike** is the foundation of this build.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Focus on Rank A Upgrade">
    Reach Rank A as quickly as possible to unlock **Stealth on Kill**. This keeps Yuna untargetable while she shreds the backline.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stack Poison Relics">
    Look for the **Poison Ascendancy** relic. This causes enemies to lose a percentage of max HP whenever they take poison damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach Rank S Mastery">
    At Rank S, Yuna gains massive **Omni-Vamp** and guaranteed crits. This allows her to solo most encounters if the rest of the guild falls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

This build scales into the millions of damage points because poison ignores traditional armor values. When combined with **Pollen's** debuff amplification, the stacks can reach over 500 on a single target, melting even the toughest Act 2 bosses in seconds.

| Build Component | Priority | Best Source | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Crit Chance** | High | Items / Relics | Triggers poison application |
| **Poison Stacks** | High | Pollen / Rank-ups | Massive DOT and max HP shred |
| **Omni-Vamp** | Medium | S-Rank Passive | Sustainability in Endless Mode |
| **Attack Speed** | Medium | Quiver Item | Faster stack application |

<Callout type="success" title="Build Synergy">
Pairing Yuna with a Rank A Pollen is the "secret sauce." Pollen's ability to increase the amount of each debuff on enemies by 100% effectively doubles Yuna's damage output instantly.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Conquering Act Bosses and Endless Mode">

The Act 1 Hydra and Act 2 Frost Dragon are the primary gear checks in **Guildrun AI**. The Hydra requires high AOE damage to manage multiple heads, while the Frost Dragon requires careful positioning to avoid its freezing breath which hits adjacent heroes.

| Boss | Main Threat | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hydra** | Multiple attacks | High poison stacks on the main body |
| **Frost Dragon** | Frost breath (AOE) | Spread heroes out; use Yuria to taunt |
| **Endless Mobs** | High HP scaling | Rely on Poison Prism and % HP damage |

Once you defeat the Frost Dragon, you enter **Endless Mode**. Here, the difficulty spikes exponentially. The key to ranking high on the global leaderboard is the **Shard Precision Prism**. By hoarding shards instead of spending them, you can push your hero's crit chance to over 700%, resulting in multiple shurikens or attacks per action.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Boss Preparation">
    Before the Frost Dragon, ensure your Tank has at least 1,500 HP and your DPS has Omni-Vamp.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Spread Formation">
    Position your tank on one side and your DPS on the other. Never group heroes together against dragons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Endless Scaling">
    Stop spending shards once your core team is Rank S. Let the shards accumulate to boost your relics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Endless Secret">
In the late stages of Endless Mode, damage numbers can reach 1.4 million per hit. At this point, defense is less important than "on-hit" healing and stealth mechanics.
</Callout>
