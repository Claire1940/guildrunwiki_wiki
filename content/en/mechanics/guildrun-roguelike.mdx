<Section icon={Map} title="Beginner Step-by-Step Strategy">

Completing your first successful run in this guildrun roguelike requires a solid understanding of resource management and team balance. New players often make the mistake of over-rolling the shop early or buying fragile damage dealers without securing a front line. Follow these structured steps to secure your first victory.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Recruit a Balanced Starting Team">
    Begin your run by covering three essential roles: a durable frontline tank, a reliable damage dealer, and a support or utility unit. Avoid filling your active slots with multiple squishy carries. If your frontline collapses, your damage dealers will be defeated before they can execute their abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check the Shop Before Spending">
    Always review the entire shop selection before committing your gold. Compare new recruits, potential duplicate upgrades, and available items. Purchasing immediate power upgrades is generally safer than holding gold for hypothetical synergies that may not appear in future shops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build Around the Strongest Offers">
    Do not force the exact same composition in every run. Adapt to the heroes, relics, and items that appear naturally. If the shop repeatedly offers class combinations or shared effects that diverge from your initial plan, pivot your strategy to capitalize on those high-tier options.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Choose Specializations With Purpose">
    As your heroes gain ranks, they unlock specialization paths. Select upgrades that directly enhance the hero's primary job. Give your tanks defensive mitigation, your carries consistent damage scaling, and your supports team-wide utility or healing frequency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Avoid aggressive shop rerolling during the first few rounds. Wasting gold early reduces your ability to purchase high-tier items and crucial specialization upgrades during the mid-game.
</Callout>

| Role | Specialization Focus | Upgrade Priority | Respec Timing |
