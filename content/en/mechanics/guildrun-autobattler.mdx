<Section icon={Swords} title="The Unstoppable guildrun autobattler Stall Strategy">

The Stall mechanic is one of the most powerful strategies in the **guildrun autobattler**. Unlike aggressive "Rush" builds that attempt to burst down enemies quickly, Stall builds focus on surviving the early seconds of combat to trigger massive late-game scaling. Stall abilities typically activate after 10, 15, 20, or 30 seconds of a fight, granting massive shields, healing, and poison damage.

The table below details the different phases of the Stall mechanic and their strategic value:

| Phase | Required Time | Active Trigger Effects | Strategic Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Early Stall** | 10 Seconds | Reflects magic damage based on armor | Survival phase transition |
| **Mid Stall** | 15 Seconds | Heals heroes in flower fields, boosts stats | Power spike for duelists |
| **Late Stall** | 20 Seconds | Grants massive max HP shields to all | Hard counter to burst damage |
| **End Stall** | 30 Seconds | Generates extra shards, massive poison debuffs | Endless mode scaling |

To make this strategy viable, players must find ways to reduce the time required to trigger these effects. By stacking specific relics and utilizing backup hero passives, you can accelerate these timers significantly.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Mastering Runs">

Achieving victory in a standard run requires careful planning from the very first node. Follow these steps to optimize your hero recruitment, economy, and combat positioning.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Starting Hero">
    Begin your run by choosing a hero with strong utility. Heroes like Pollen are excellent choices, as they create flower fields that apply poison and generate shields based on your magic power. This provides a safe foundation for your early-game fights.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish Your Economy">
    Focus on accumulating Shards (gold) during the first three combats. Avoid rerolling the shop early. Look for relics or choices that grant bonus Shards or reduce the cost of items in the shop. A strong economy allows you to purchase high-tier heroes later.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assemble Your Core Synergy">
    As you progress past the first boss, pivot into your chosen archetype. If you find defensive relics, commit to a Stall build by recruiting tanks like Scorn and Pimenta. If you find attack speed items, transition into a Duelist-heavy composition.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Board Positioning">
    Position your main tank in the front-center hex to draw enemy aggro. Place your damage dealers two hexes behind to protect them from area-of-effect (AoE) attacks. Ensure your support units are close enough to apply shields to your tank.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Backup Hero Mechanics">
Remember that certain hero abilities work from the backup bench, meaning you can benefit from their stats and passive traits without placing them on the active board.
</Callout>
