<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Gameplay Loop and Launch Day Features">

At its heart, Guildrun combines the drafting tension of an autobattler with the high-stakes decision-making of a roguelike. Every run requires you to build a cohesive guild of heroes from random shop offerings, adapt to the items and relics you find, and position your forces to counter specific enemy formations.

The full release will expand the roster to 25 distinct heroes. These heroes are not locked into rigid roles; instead, they can be customized using 75 specialization paths and 180 individual specializations.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Difficulty Progression and Endless Mode Strategies">

For players seeking a true test of their tactical planning, Guildrun features an escalating difficulty system culminating in a brutal Endless Mode. The game features eight distinct difficulty levels, each introducing new modifiers, smarter enemy AI, and unique boss tokens that must be managed.

To successfully navigate the highest difficulties, you must master the art of the pivot. Sticking to a single, predetermined team composition will often lead to failure if the shop does not offer the exact units or relics you need.
