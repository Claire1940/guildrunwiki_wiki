<Section icon={Swords} title="The Logan 'One-Shot' Battle Mage Build">

Logan is a unique unit that bridges the gap between a Warrior and a Mage. His ability to deal massive physical damage while benefiting from magic scaling makes him the centerpiece of any advanced **guildrun strategy**. To maximize his potential, you must focus on his **Execution** mechanic, which grants him permanent attack and crit bonuses upon securing a kill.

To build the ultimate Logan carry, follow these specific gear and ranking priorities:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Game Stacking">
    Focus on obtaining Logan early and giving him items that provide **Omnivamp**. His survival depends on his ability to heal through the damage he takes while charging his active ability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlocking Rush">
    Promote Logan to A-Rank as quickly as possible to unlock the **Rush** trigger. This allows him to gain 100% increased attack during his rush window, which is essential for one-shotting high-HP targets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Late Game Scaling">
    Equip Logan with the **Crown** or other epic relics that boost attack speed. By floor 10, Logan should have enough permanent crit and attack stacks to clear entire waves with a single cast.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Item | Best Stats | Logan Synergy | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Omnivamp Blade** | Life Steal / Attack | Keeps Logan alive during Execution | High |
| **Magic Staff** | Mana Regen / Magic | Faster ability cycling | Medium |
| **Warrior Medallion** | Flat Damage | Boosts all Warrior-class attacks | High |
| **Crit Cloak** | Crit Chance / Speed | Triggers execution more reliably | Essential |

<Callout type="warning" title="Glass Cannon Risk">
While Logan can deal millions of damage, he is susceptible to being focused down if your frontline fails. Ensure you have a dedicated tank like **Scorn** or a high-shielding unit like **Kai** to draw aggro away from him.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Relic Prioritization and Shard Economy">

Managing your shards (the in-game currency) is the difference between a failed run and a record-breaking victory. A disciplined **guildrun strategy** involves knowing when to roll the shop and when to save for epic relics in the auction house.

**Top Relics for Warrior Compositions:**
- **Protection Crystal:** Essential for early survival; provides defense and Max HP to adjacent allies.
- **Warrior's Medallion:** A flat 25% damage boost to all Warriors; a must-have for Logan-centric teams.
- **Amulet of Shields:** Increases all shield gains by 50%, significantly boosting Kai's damage output.
- **Shattering Chasm:** An advanced relic that causes shields to explode when they expire or are broken.
