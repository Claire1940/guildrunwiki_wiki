The shop offers a randomized selection, but you have tools to influence the outcome. <Section icon={Zap} title="Hero Ranking and Ability Evolution">

One of the most critical aspects of the **guildrun shop guide** is the ranking system. Unlike traditional RPGs where heroes level up through XP, Guildrun requires you to purchase duplicates from the shop to increase their rank. Ranking up a hero significantly boosts their base stats and, more importantly, unlocks new tiers of abilities.

When you reach Rank A or S, you are often given a choice between different upgrades. For example, a Mage might choose between "Searing Flames" (Burn focus) or "Glacial Touch" (Frost focus). These choices should align with your existing Relics. If you have the **Burn Catalyst** relic, always choose the fire-based upgrade to maximize damage output.

| Rank Level | Stat Increase | Ability Changes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **B → A** | +25% Base Stats | Unlocks first passive trait |
| **A → S** | +50% Base Stats | Unlocks ultimate/secondary class |
| **S+ (Endless)** | Incremental | Stat scaling based on hero type |

<Callout type="success" title="Synergy Bonus">
Ranking up a hero who shares a class with your leader (like a Mage leader with Mage recruits) triggers the **Class Synergy** bonus, providing free Magic or Defense stats to everyone in that class.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Optimize Your Shop Visit">

To maximize your efficiency, you should follow a specific workflow every time you enter the shop. Blindly buying the most expensive hero is a common mistake that leads to a lack of synergy.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check Synergies First">
    Scan the three available heroes for icons that match your current team's classes. Look for "Mage," "Vanguard," or "Assassin" tags.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Evaluate Relics">
    Check the Relic slot. If there is a relic that buffs your primary damage type (e.g., Burn or Frost), buy it immediately. Relics are rare and often more valuable than a single hero rank.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Inventory">
    Drag items from the shop onto specific heroes. Give HP items to your Vanguards and Magic items to your Mages. You can unequip and swap items at any time without cost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reroll or Freeze">
    If you have excess shards and haven't found a duplicate hero, reroll once. If a vital hero appears but you are broke, hit the **Freeze** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Inventory Management">
Be careful not to sell items or heroes accidentally. While selling provides a small shard refund, it is rarely worth losing a ranked-up unit unless you are completely pivoting your strategy.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Items, Relics, and Keywords">

The shop isn't just for heroes; it's where you define your "build" through specialized gear. Understanding keywords like **Rush**, **Stall**, and **Backup** is essential for high-level play.

- **Rush**: Effects that trigger in the first 15 seconds of battle. Ideal for aggressive, high-damage teams.
- **Stall**: Effects that activate only after 20 seconds. Perfect for tanky compositions that outlast the enemy.
- **Backup**: These items or abilities work even if the hero is in your reserve slots, allowing you to buff your active team from the sidelines.

| Item/Relic Name | Type | Key Effect | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Patient Ring** | Item | +100 Max HP, +12 Magic | Frontline Mages |
| **Burn Catalyst** | Relic | +33% Burn Damage | Fire-based teams |
| **Mage Robes** | Relic | +25 Magic to all Mages | Magic-heavy comps |
| **Plated Codex** | Relic | +10 Defense per victory | Long-term scaling |
| **Shard Lattice** | Relic | Increases Shard gain | Early game economy |

<Callout type="tip" title="The Auction House">
After defeating a boss, you will enter the **Auction House**. This is a special version of the shop that offers higher rarity items and the rare ability to **Increase Team Size**. Always save at least 20-30 shards before a boss fight to afford these elite upgrades.
</Callout>
