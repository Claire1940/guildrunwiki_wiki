The shop offers three heroes per visit. Buying a hero you already own will rank them up, unlocking new active and passive abilities. For example, ranking up a Mage might grant the "Mystic" secondary class, significantly increasing mana regeneration.

| Action | Shard Cost | Strategic Value | When to Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Single Reroll** | 2 Shards | Moderate | When looking for a specific rank-up |
| **Freeze Shop** | 0 Shards | High | When you see a hero you can't afford yet |
| **Sell Hero** | + Variable | Low | Only to reach a shard milestone for a Relic |
| **Rank Up** | Variable | Essential | Always prioritize ranking up your core carry |

<Callout type="warning" title="Shard Conservation">

<Section icon={Zap} title="Class Synergies and Skill Scaling">

Success in *Guildrun* is determined by how well your heroes interact. A core component of any **guildrun reroll guide** is identifying which classes to stack. Mages, for instance, gain massive power when paired with other Mages, especially if you find relics that amplify debuffs like Burn or Frost.

  <Step num="1" title="Identify Core Synergy">
    Choose a primary damage type (e.g., Burn). Look for heroes like Funky who apply this status automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Support Classes">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Itemization and Relic Management">

Items strengthen individual heroes, while Relics affect the whole guild. In the context of a **guildrun reroll guide**, you should prioritize Relics that provide permanent scaling. The "Plated Codex," for example, increases defense permanently after every victory, making it a top-tier early-game find.

**Top Tier Relics for Reroll Priority:**
1. **Burn Catalyst**: Essential for fire-based teams; amplifies all burn ticks.
2. **Mage Robes**: Provides flat Magic and Mana Regen to all casters.
3. **Emergency Rewind**: A safety net relic that grants shards upon defeat.
4. **Shard Lattice**: Increases shard gains from combat, allowing for more frequent rerolls.

</Callout>
