<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Keywords: Rush, Stall, and Backup">

To master the late game, you must understand the timing-based keywords that dictate when certain abilities activate. These keywords allow you to build teams that excel at different phases of a battle.

- **Rush**: These effects are only active during the first 15 seconds of battle. They are perfect for fast, aggressive teams looking to end fights quickly.
- **Stall**: These effects activate only after 20 seconds have passed. This is the realm of tanks and healers who can outlast the enemy.
- **Backup**: These effects work even if the hero is in your reserve slots. This allows you to gain team-wide buffs without taking up a valuable spot on the battlefield.

Understanding the "Stall" mechanic is particularly important for the demo boss. The boss has high health, meaning the fight will inevitably last longer than 20 seconds. Equipping items or choosing passive upgrades that trigger during the Stall phase will give you a massive advantage when the boss is at half health.

| Keyword | Timing | Best Hero Class | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rush** | 0-15s | Assassin / Burst Mage | End fights before taking damage |
| **Standard** | 15-20s | All | The peak of ability rotations |
| **Stall** | 20s+ | Vanguard / Support | Out-sustain the opponent |
| **Backup** | Passive | Any | Global stat boosts for the team |

<Callout type="tip" title="Backup Strategy">
Even if you find a hero that doesn't fit your active combat grid, check if they have a "Backup" keyword. A hero in reserve providing +10 Magic to all Mages is often worth keeping.
</Callout>
