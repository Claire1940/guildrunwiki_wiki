<Section icon={Shield} title="Positioning and Survival Tactics">

In SSS difficulty, positioning can mean the difference between an effortless victory and an instant defeat. Since enemies deal extreme damage, you cannot rely on a standard tank standing in the front row. Instead, you must utilize stealth mechanics to manipulate enemy targeting behaviors.

By placing your entire team in the back two rows, you force the enemy frontline to walk forward, wasting precious seconds of their combat buffs.

| Position | Unit | Primary Purpose | Stealth Benefit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Back Row Left** | Yuna | Safe DPS & Shard Generation | Immune to early target locks |
| **Back Row Right** | Tilly | Infinite Scaling DPS Carry | Uninterrupted attack speed scaling |
| **Back Row Center** | Dagamir | Off-Tank / Burst Assassin | Drops aggro to main tank |
| **Mid Row Center** | Rip | Main Vanguard Tank | Taunts enemies after 10 seconds |

<Callout type="warning" title="Aggro Management">
Never place your primary carry adjacent to your main tank. If the enemy uses area-of-effect abilities, your carry will take massive splash damage and die prematurely.
</Callout>

Keep your units clustered slightly to maximize the efficiency of adjacent shield relics, but ensure your squishiest damage dealers are positioned furthest from the primary target zone.
