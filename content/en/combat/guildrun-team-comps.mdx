<Section icon={Target} title="Hero Classes and Stat Scaling">

Every hero in your **guildrun team comps** has a specific class that dictates their growth. Understanding these classes is vital for choosing the right items in the shop. For example, a Warrior gains health and attack upon leveling up, while a Duelist focuses on health and attack speed.

The iconography on the left side of a hero card is not just flavor text. If you see a purple icon next to a damage number, that ability scales with Magic. If you see a red Shuriken icon, it scales with Crit. 

<Callout type="warning" title="Mana Pool Trap">
Do not assume a larger mana pool is better. In Guildrun, a hero with a 30-mana pool will cast their ability much more frequently than a hero with an 80-mana pool, assuming equal mana regeneration.
</Callout>

| Class | Base Strengths | Typical Scaling | Best Position |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Warrior** | High HP, Shielding | Attack, Defense | Front Row |
| **Duelist** | Attack Speed, Multi-hit | Attack Speed, Magic | Middle/Back |
| **Assassin** | Burst, Stealth | Crit, Crit Damage | Flank/Back |
| **Mage** | AOE, Spells | Magic, Mana Regen | Back Row |
| **Vanguard** | Pure Tanking | Health, Armor | Front Row |
| **Mystic** | Healing, Shielding | Magic, Mana Regen | Reserve/Back |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Team Construction">

Building a competitive team is a process of iteration. You cannot simply pick your final team in Act 1; you must adapt to the heroes and relics offered in the shop.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Starter">
    Choose a hero that fits your preferred playstyle. Yuna is great for early burst, while Logan offers more sustainability through shields.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Your Carry">
    By the end of Act 1, decide which hero will receive your best items. This hero should be the one you level to A or S tier first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Hunt for Key Relics">
    Visit the Auction House after the first Act boss. Look for "Harmony Crystals" to increase your team size or class-specific banners like the "Duelist Toxin Banner."
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize the Reserve">
    Recruit heroes with passive backup abilities. A Mystic in the reserve can provide healing to your active party without taking up a combat slot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Finalize Synergies">
    Before entering Endless Mode, ensure your carry has capped their primary stat (like 100% Crit) to trigger secondary effects like Crit Damage scaling.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="The Auction House">
The Auction House is a special shop that appears after Act bosses. It contains higher-tier relics and the essential Harmony Crystals needed to expand your active team size from 3 to 5.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Endgame Survival and Endless Mode">

Survival in this mode requires more than just damage; you need defensive layers like lifesteal (Omnivamp) and crowd control (Stuns/Frost).
