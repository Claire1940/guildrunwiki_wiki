<Section icon={Zap} title="Best Class Synergies and Keywords">

Success in Guildrun isn't just about picking high-tier heroes; it's about how their keywords interact. The two most prominent playstyles currently involve **Rush** (early fight dominance) and **Stall** (scaling power over time).

### The Burn & Magic Synergy
By pairing **Funky** with other fire-inflicting units, you can trigger the **Burn Catalyst** relic. This relic increases damage by 33% for every stack of burn applied. When combined with the **Mage** passive that grants permanent magic on cast, your team's power ceiling becomes virtually limitless.

### The Frost & Control Synergy
If you find yourself struggling with high-damage bosses, transitioning into a **Frost** build with units like **Arya** can provide the necessary crowd control. Frost slows enemy attack speed, giving your **Vanguards** more time to regenerate health and your Mages more time to build mana.

| Synergy Type | Core Keywords | Best Relic | Primary Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Firestorm** | Burn, Mage, Rush | Burn Catalyst | Massive AoE DPS |
| **Deep Freeze** | Frost, Mystic, Stall | Glacial Shard | Crowd Control |
| **Iron Wall** | Vanguard, Shield, Defense | Plated Codex | High Survivability |
| **Shadow Arts** | Assassin, Stealth, Rush | Hidden Blade | Single Target Burst |

<Callout type="warning" title="Stall Keyword Warning">
**Stall** effects only activate after the first 20 seconds of combat. If your team is too squishy, you will be defeated before your most powerful abilities even trigger. Ensure you have at least one high-HP Vanguard to bridge the gap.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Building a Winning Team">

Building a perfect guild requires a balance between spending shards for immediate power and saving for high-tier Auction House items. Follow these steps to optimize your run progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Hero Selection">
    Start with **Funky** or a hero with a strong AoE ability. This ensures you can clear the first few trash mobs quickly to accumulate shards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Your Core Synergy">
    By the third shop visit, look at your available relics. If you see the **Burn Catalyst**, commit to a fire-based Mage build. If you see defensive relics, pivot to a Vanguard-heavy frontline.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Team Size">
    Always save shards for the **Auction House** after boss fights. Increasing your **Team Size** is the single most impactful upgrade you can purchase, as it allows for more synergy triggers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Rank Up Key Units">
    Don't ignore duplicates. Ranking a hero to **Rank S** unlocks unique passives that can change their secondary class, such as turning a pure Mage into a **Mystic** hybrid.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Item Distribution">
    Equip **Robes** on your Mages for magic scaling and **Rings** or **Heavy Armor** on your Vanguards. Items can be unequipped at any time, so don't be afraid to move them as you recruit better heroes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Backup Mechanic">
Remember that some heroes have **Backup** abilities. These units provide bonuses to your active team even while sitting in the reserve slots. This is a great way to utilize low-rank heroes who share a class with your starters.
</Callout>

</Section>
