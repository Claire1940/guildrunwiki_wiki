Ranking heroes in Guildrun requires looking at their "Rank Up" potential. A hero's base stats are secondary to the specializations they unlock at B-rank and beyond. Our tier list focuses on heroes that provide the highest return on investment during high-difficulty runs.

| **Tilly (Rank C)** | C | Warrior | Low base impact without attack speed items |

**S-Tier: The Core Carries**
Tilly remains the undisputed queen of the **guildrun hero tier list**. Her ability to gain attack speed and damage every time she generates shards allows her to outscale bosses in seconds. When paired with the "Stylish" specialization, she deals bonus damage per shard generated throughout the entire game.

**A-Tier: Strong Synergizers**
Dragonmir is the perfect secondary carry. His "Devour" mechanic generates shards on kills, which can then be consumed to permanently increase his Omnivamp. This makes him an excellent choice for the "Vanguard" transition if you need more frontline survivability.

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Tip">

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Guide">

Navigating a run from the first combat to the Endless Mode transition requires careful planning at the Campfire and Auction House.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Draft">
    Start with Tilly or a strong Tank. Focus on winning the first six combats to secure the 50-shard bonus, which jumpstarts your economy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The B-Rank Pivot">
    Once your core heroes reach B-rank, select specializations that complement your items. For Tilly, choose "Stylish." For Zury, choose "Serpent's Kiss."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Auction House Strategy">
    After defeating the Act One boss, enter the Auction House. Prioritize the **Harmony Crystal** to increase team size or high-tier relics that trigger on shard generation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Endless Transition">
    In Endless Mode, focus on "Execute" mechanics. Use the Assassin class bonus to instantly kill enemies below 35% HP, as enemy health scales faster than your raw damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Campfire Advice">
Don't just use the Campfire to rank up heroes. If your **Emergency Rewind** is broken, recharging it is often more valuable than a 10% stat boost, especially before a boss fight.
</Callout>

</Section>

**Relic Synergy**
The most "busted" relics are those that interact with shard generation. Some relics allow you to deal 10% of an enemy's max health as damage every time a shard is generated. In a Tilly-focused build, this can trigger multiple times per second, melting even the tankiest Endless Mode bosses.
