<Section icon={Map} title="Item Mechanics: Rush vs. Stall">

Understanding the combat timer is essential for the **guildrun item tier list**. Items generally fall into two categories: **Rush** items that trigger at the start of the fight, and **Stall** items that trigger after a certain duration.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Combat Length">
    Check the enemy lineup. If they are glass cannons (Assassins), prioritize **Rush** items like the Hasty Vest to survive the first 10 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Scaling Gear">
    Against tanky Slimes or Turtles, equip **Stall** gear. Items that trigger after 20 or 30 seconds provide much higher stat ceilings for long battles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Balance Mana Regen">
    Ensure your active ability heroes (Mages/Assassins) have at least 10-15 Mana Regen. Without this, they may only cast once per fight, wasting their potential.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Shard Generation">
    In early rounds, prioritize items that generate shards on kill. This extra gold allows you to buy S-Tier items from the Auction House later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Mechanic | Trigger Time | Best Use Case | Key Item |
|----------|--------------|---------------|----------|
| **Rush** | 0-10 Seconds | Fast Mob Clears | Hasty Vest |
| **Stall** | 15+ Seconds | Boss Battles | Apotheosis |
| **Backup** | Passive | Bench Support | Pollen's Sworn |
| **Lasting** | Permanent | Infinite Shields | Kai's Resolute |

<Callout type="info" title="Mechanic Insight">
**Lasting Shields** do not expire after the standard 10-second window. This makes them significantly more valuable than standard shields, especially when running a solo-tank composition with Rowan.
</Callout>
