<Section icon={Target} title="Positioning and Combat Strategy">

Proper positioning is what separates a successful run from an early defeat. Since assassins rely on stealth to avoid direct confrontation, your frontline must be positioned to draw all initial enemy aggro.

| Row | Hero | Primary Duty | Positioning Logic |
| Front Row | Dragamir | Solo Tanking | Draws initial melee aggro, activates Devour |
| Back Row | Nyx | Safe DPS | Gains 100% crit and 5s stealth immediately |
| Back Row | Logan | Buffing Nyx | Placed adjacent to Nyx to share passive AD buffs |
| Reserve | Yuna | Backup Poison | Triggers backup assassin poison buffs from the bench |

When facing bosses with heavy area-of-effect (AoE) attacks, space your backline assassins apart. Keeping Nyx and Logan on opposite sides of the back row prevents them from being hit by the same sweeping spells, ensuring at least one carry remains healthy throughout the fight.
