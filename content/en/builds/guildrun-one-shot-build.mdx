The foundational mechanic of this strategy is the crit overcap system. Unlike traditional RPGs where critical strike chance caps at 100%, this system converts any excess crit percentage directly into critical damage. For example, reaching a 400% critical strike chance translates into a massive critical damage multiplier, allowing your primary damage dealers to instantly defeat elite enemies.

Stealth serves as both a defensive safeguard and an offensive catalyst. When your assassins drop below specific health thresholds, or when they secure a kill, they enter stealth. While stealthed, they are untargetable, regenerate mana rapidly, and permanently scale their critical strike stats for the remainder of the combat encounter.

<Callout type="tip" title="Overcap Math">
Every 1% of critical strike chance acquired above the 100% threshold increases your total critical strike damage by a proportional value. Prioritize flat crit items even after reaching the baseline cap.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Core Hero Lineup & Upgrade Paths">

Building a successful high-burst team requires selecting units that naturally scale their offensive stats during combat. Your frontline must buy enough time for your backline assassins to trigger their scaling mechanics. The table below outlines the optimal roster for this specific strategy.

| Hero | Role | Key Synergy | Rating |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Yuna** | Main Physical DPS | Stealth-on-kill & crit scaling | ★★★★★ |
| **Ratna** | Main Magical DPS | Low-health execution & stun on crit | ★★★★★ |
| **Zori** | Scaling Frontline | Permanent defense & magic scaling | ★★★★☆ |
| **Logan** | Flex Assassin | Permanent attack scaling on kill | ★★★★☆ |

Yuna functions as your primary physical damage dealer. Her active ability allows her to leap into the air and throw high-damage projectiles at the lowest-health enemy. When upgraded to S-rank, she gains temporary stealth upon securing a kill, which feeds back into her permanent crit scaling loop.

Ratna acts as the magical counterpart, targeting weak backline units and stunning them on critical hits. Her ability to execute targets ensures that Yuna's stealth triggers remain active throughout the fight. Zori holds the frontline, scaling her defenses up to five times per combat, ensuring the glass-cannon assassins remain undisturbed.

<Callout type="warning" title="Frontline Stability">
Without Zori scaling her defenses early in the round, your assassins will be targeted immediately. Do not neglect your frontline upgrades in favor of pure damage.
