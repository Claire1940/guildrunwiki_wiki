<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Mechanics of the guildrun kai build">

Kai operates as a premier scaling carry in *Guildrun*. His primary mechanic revolves around charging a mighty attack that grants a massive temporary shield, which then explodes to deal bonus damage directly proportional to his current shield value. This creates a unique defensive-offensive feedback loop where survival stats directly boost your offensive output.

- Highlights the power of the Tank Glyph combined with the Vanguard class.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="GimB7p3ZEVQ" title="Kai, Stacking Frankly Stupid Amounts of Shield | Guildrun" />

During his active ability's 2-second charge time, Kai is highly vulnerable. Securing early shield generation or high flat defense is crucial to surviving this window. Once the charge completes, Kai gains a massive shield and releases a spin attack. The spin attack deals base damage plus bonus damage equal to his current shield. If he has 2,000 shield, he deals massive AoE damage. This makes shield scaling the absolute priority for this build.

Furthermore, shields in *Guildrun* last for 10 seconds unless specified as "lasting shields". This means you must continuously cycle active abilities and leverage passive triggers to maintain your defensive buffer. When enemies attack Kai's shields, specific relics can reflect that damage back, turning his defense into a passive weapon.

<Callout type="tip" title="Ramping Shield Power">
Kai gains attack power whenever he is shielded or when his shields take damage. Focus on rapid shield application rather than single large shields to maximize this attack-stacking passive.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Best Items and Relics for Kai">

To optimize the **guildrun kai build**, you must balance active combat items on Kai with powerful backup relics placed on your benched heroes. Kai himself needs a mix of survivability, shield amplification, and raw stats. Items like the Harmony Crystal, Vanguard emblems, and defense-stacking glyphs are crucial.

|------|------|--------|--------|
| **Tank Glyph** | Class Emblem | Grants Tank class, +90 Defense, enables defense-to-attack scaling | ★★★★★ |
| **Harmony Crystal** | Stat Relic | Increases primary stats based on active classes | ★★★★☆ |
| **Squire's Sword** | Weapon | Increases basic attack speed and grants reliable omnivamp | ★★★★☆ |
| **Blizzard Ward** | Utility | Spawns a blizzard every second, applying frost to lower enemy attack speed | ★★★★☆ |

While active items keep Kai alive on the front lines, your bench setup dictates your late-game scaling. Backup-only relics allow benched heroes to pass massive flat defensive values directly to Kai without clogging up your active board space.

| Relic | Placement | Team Benefit | Rating |
|------|------|--------|--------|
| **Backup Shield Emblem** | Bench Only | Start of combat: Gives all active heroes 1,500 lasting shields | ★★★★★ |
| **Vanguard Reserve** | Bench Only | Grants 200 Max HP per rank of Vanguard units in reserves | ★★★★☆ |
| **Tank Reserve** | Bench Only | Grants 30 Defense per rank of Tank units in reserves | ★★★★★ |
| **Investment Shard** | Bench Only | Generates mana regen and shards over time through passive investment | ★★★★☆ |

<Callout type="warning" title="Backup Relic Placement">
Backup-only relics do nothing if the holder is on the active board. Always ensure these units are placed in your reserve slots to activate their team-wide defensive buffs.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Progression Guide">

Building this setup requires careful planning across the different acts of a run. Follow these steps to transition smoothly from an early-game carry to an unstoppable endless-mode tank.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Game Setup">
    Acquire Kai and focus on basic attack speed and omnivamp. Do not rush a full team; focus resources on hyper-carrying Kai through the first few acts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Transitioning to Shield Stacking">
    Pick up Mystic and Vanguard supports. Look for items that grant shields on cast or spawn protective flower fields to keep Kai healthy during his active ability charge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimizing the Bench">
    Purchase high-rank units (A-rank or S-rank) and place them on the bench with backup-only relics. This passes massive passive defense to Kai, allowing him to tank high-damage bosses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Endless Mode Scaling">
    Acquire sandstorm protection and frost-inflicting relics to neutralize backline damage dealers (like mushroom snipers) in high-tier endless runs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

As you progress, keep an eye out for stall triggers. Relics that grant defense and magic for every stall trigger will scale your team's survivability exponentially during prolonged boss fights.

<Callout type="info" title="Stall Triggers">
Stall triggers activate when a combat round lasts longer than a specified duration. These are highly beneficial for shield builds, as they reward defensive playstyles with permanent stat growth.
</Callout>
