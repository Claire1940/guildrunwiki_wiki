<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Setup for a Guildrun Magic Build">

Executing a perfect **guildrun magic build** requires careful planning from the very first shop visit. Follow these steps to ensure your team scales correctly through the eight difficulty levels.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Recruit Your Magical Core">
    Start with one reliable Ranged hero and one sturdy Tank. Do not over-recruit damage dealers early; you need a balanced foundation to survive the first three rifts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Ability Scaling">
    In the first few shops, look for items that increase Ability Power or Attack Speed. If a "Class-Scaling" relic appears, commit to that specific magical path immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Mid-Tier Specializations">
    Once you reach Rank B, select specializations that offer AoE damage or crowd control. This will help you clear large enemy groups in the middle stages of the run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize the Frontline">
    As your magic damage increases, the enemies will live longer. Invest in your Tank's defensive specializations to ensure they can hold the line while your mages cast.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Finalize the S-Rank Pivot">
    In the late game, use your Shards to hunt for S-rank specializations that provide "Infinite Scaling" or "Execute" mechanics to handle Difficulty 8 bosses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Positioning Tip">
Always place your magic carry in the "Protected" slot. Even the strongest magic build will fail if the main damage source is targeted by an assassin in the first two seconds.
</Callout>
