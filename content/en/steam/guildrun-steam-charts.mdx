<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimization Steps for High-Floor Runs">

Reaching double-digit floors requires a specific sequence of upgrades and shop management. Players who consistently appear at the top of the **guildrun steam charts** leaderboards follow a strict progression path to ensure their economy and unit ranks stay ahead of the difficulty curve.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Game Shard Management">
    Focus on acquiring a **Shard Weaver** early. This allows you to generate extra shards during combat, which is vital for late-game rerolls and high-rank unit purchases.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Stability System Selection">
    When presented with the choice, the **Stability System** is often superior to the **Emergency Rerun**. It provides a more consistent power floor, allowing you to save your rerolls for critical S-Rank units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Warrior Medallion Acquisition">
    Since the current meta heavily favors Warriors, obtaining the **Warriors Medallion** (+25 damage) early can carry your mid-game. Every Warrior in your team gains this flat bonus, which is massive for multi-hit units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Finalizing the Shield Core">
    By Floor 8, you must have the **Shield Converter** or **Amulet of Shields**. This converts your defensive stats into a secondary damage source, which is the only way to melt the high HP pools of Floor 10+ bosses.
  </Step>
</Steps>
